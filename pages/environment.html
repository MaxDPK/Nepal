Environment — Air Pollution

Kathmandu faces severe air-quality challenges—especially in winter—due to vehicles, brick kilns, burning, and road dust.

  • Government actions: stricter emissions, EV incentives, cleaner brick-kiln tech.
  • Road paving and dust control projects around the Valley.
  • Air-quality monitoring and public awareness with NGOs and partners.
