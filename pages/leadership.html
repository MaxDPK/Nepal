Leadership — Pushpa Kamal Dahal (Prachanda)

From leading a decade-long insurgency to guiding the republic—Prachanda is a pivotal, controversial figure in modern Nepal.

  • Led the Maoist movement (1996–2006) that ended the monarchy.
  • Key role in peace process and 2015 constitution; advocates inclusive federalism.
  • Faced challenges: political instability, development, earthquake recovery, foreign relations.
