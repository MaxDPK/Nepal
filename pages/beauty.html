Beauty Standards

Traditional ideals—clear skin, natural hair, elegant dress—meet modern trends from Bollywood, K-pop, and Instagram.

  • Women: saris/kurta, minimal makeup; growing focus on glowing skin & styled looks.
  • Men: taller, styled hair/beards; gym culture rising.
  • Natural remedies like turmeric and sandalwood remain popular.
