Entertainment — Dohori

Dohori is a fast-witted, back-and-forth musical debate—questions and replies sung in rhyme.

  • Teams—often men vs women—improvise lyrics on love, rural life, and social issues.
  • Originated in village gatherings; now popular on radio/TV with long contests.
  • Blends melody, humor, and cultural pride.
