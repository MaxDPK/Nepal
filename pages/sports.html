Sports — Dandi Biyo

A uniquely Nepali village sport using a long stick (dandi) and a short stick (biyo).

  • Players flip the biyo up and hit it for distance—quick reflexes needed.
  • Once considered the national game; still played during festivals.
  • Simple gear, big fun—creativity of rural life.
