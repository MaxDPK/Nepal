Language

Nepali—an Indo-Aryan language written in Devanagari—is Nepal’s official language, but the country is home to 120+ languages.

  • Spoken natively by 17M+; used in government, education, and media.
  • Developed from Sanskrit; influenced by many regional languages.
  • Spread during 18th-century unification under Prithvi Narayan Shah.
  • Immense diversity: 120+ languages and dialects across Nepal.
