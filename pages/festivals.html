Festivals — Holi (Fagu Purnima)

Holi marks spring’s arrival and the victory of good over evil—celebrated with color, water, music, and joy.

  • Observed over two days: first in the Hills (incl. Kathmandu), next in the Terai.
  • Starts with raising the Chir pole at Basantapur; ends with its burning.
  • A time for friendship, renewal, and community spirit.
