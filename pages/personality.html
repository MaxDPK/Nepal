Personality — Nepal’s Profile

Survey data suggests Nepal leans slightly introverted, feeling, prospecting, and more turbulent than assertive.

  • Mind: Slightly more Introverted than Extraverted.
  • Energy: Skews toward Intuitive thinking about ideas and possibilities.
  • Nature: Preference for Feeling—valuing empathy and harmony.
  • Tactics: More Prospecting than Judging—flexible and adaptable.
  • Identity: More Turbulent—self-reflective and improvement-oriented.
