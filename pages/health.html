Health — Ayurveda & Herbs

Natural healing—Ayurveda and local herbal knowledge—remains part of daily wellness in Nepal.

  • Common herbs: Tulsi (respiratory), Ashwagandha (stress), Turmeric (anti-inflammatory), Neem (skin/blood).
  • Used as teas, powders, oils; often paired with yoga and meditation.
  • Traditional healers (Baidhya) and modern Ayurvedic clinics both active.
