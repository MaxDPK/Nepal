Population

Nepal’s population is young, diverse, and spread from the Terai plains to the high Himalayas.

  • ~30 million people; most live in the Terai and hill regions.
  • Young demographic; urban migration to Kathmandu, Pokhara, Biratnagar.
  • Significant labor migration abroad—remittances support families and the economy.
  • Over 120 languages and many ethnic communities shape culture and festivals.
  • Challenges: urban crowding, youth employment, rural access to health/education.
